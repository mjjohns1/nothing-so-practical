## About

We are a group of behavioral scientists, data scientists, epidemiologists and research methodologists.
We write about a range of topics, mostly related to quantitative methods. This includes causal inference,
Bayesian statistics, study design, and measurement. Occasionally, we might veer off into an interesting
data science project or philosophy piece.

All the opinions expressed are those of the authors and do not necessarily reflect the official policy or
position of any agency or employer.
